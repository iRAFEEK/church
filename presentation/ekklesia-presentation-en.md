# WHAT IS ALREADY BUILT AND WORKING TODAY

## Slide 5 — Never Lose a Visitor Again

### From First Visit to Full Member — Automatically Tracked

- Print a single QR code and place it at your church entrance. Visitors scan and fill a simple form on their phone.
- The moment they submit, your leadership is notified and a WhatsApp welcome message goes out automatically.
- Each visitor is assigned to a leader with a 48-hour follow-up deadline — configurable per church.
- If nobody follows up in time, the system escalates automatically with red alerts on the admin dashboard.
- When a visitor is ready, convert them to a full member with one click. All their data carries over.

**Speaker Notes:**
Walk through the flow: visitor scans QR at the entrance, fills out the form in 30 seconds, gets a WhatsApp welcome within minutes, a leader is assigned, and follow-up is tracked with a countdown timer. The key insight: no visitor silently disappears anymore. The system holds your team accountable — lovingly, but firmly.

## Slide 6 — Know Who Is Falling Away Before It Is Too Late

### At-Risk Detection: The Safety Net Your Church Needs

- Attendance is taken in under 60 seconds per group meeting — tap the names who showed up, done.
- The system automatically detects members with 2 or more consecutive absences and flags them as "at risk."
- Group leaders see at-risk members prominently on their dashboard with the last date they were seen.
- Leaders can log follow-up actions: "Called on Tuesday, spoke with them, they are traveling."
- The admin dashboard shows at-risk members across the entire church — no blind spots for leadership.

**Speaker Notes:**
Emphasize the 60-second attendance flow. Group leaders will not use a tool that slows them down. This was designed so a leader can take attendance on their phone during the first five minutes of a gathering. The real value is what happens with that data — the system turns attendance records into pastoral action. You will know who is drifting before they are gone.

- WhatsApp is the primary notification channel — not email that nobody reads.
- Also supports email, push notifications, and in-app notifications — four channels total.
- Each member chooses their preferred channel. The system routes messages accordingly.
- Automated triggers send gathering reminders 24 hours before, visitor assignment alerts, at-risk notifications, and serving invitations — all without manual effort.

## Slide 9 — Events Without the Chaos

### From Sunday Service to Special Events — Organized

- Create events from reusable templates — weekly services, conferences, community nights, holiday programs.
- Members register in the app. Non-members register via a public link and are automatically added to your visitor pipeline.
- Check-in on the day of the event with a simple tap interface.
- Service staffing: assign volunteers to roles (worship, sound, ushers, children's ministry), send WhatsApp invitations, and track who accepted or declined.
- See confirmed versus needed coverage for every serving team before the event — not the morning of.

**Speaker Notes:**

They accept or decline in the app. The coordinator sees a real-time dashboard showing who is confirmed and where there are gaps — days before the event, not during the scramble on Sunday morning.
